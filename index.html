Get Percentage Alexa Skill

The "Get Percentage" Alexa Skill can be used to calculate discounts, taxes, tips or help you with homework as well. Just ask Alexa - What is result of twenty percent of one twenty and Alexa will calculate the difference between the original value and the percent to return the final result. Apart from that, you can also work with regular percentages by asking "thirty five percent of hundred thousand" to get the result.
